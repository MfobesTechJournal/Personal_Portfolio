About Me

Developer. Engineer.
Problem Solver.

Full-stack developer and data engineer based in Johannesburg, South Africa. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, data, and cloud infrastructure — I build production-ready systems that solve real problems, whether that is an AI-powered job matcher, an automated ETL pipeline, or a multi-cloud deployment with CI/CD automation.

My technical foundation was established through a B.Tech in Project Management at Cape Peninsula University of Technology and a Certificate in Full Stack Development at FNB App Academy. I am equally comfortable integrating generative AI APIs — OpenAI, Gemini, and Groq — into production applications.
